Dashboard Overview

Total Leads iTotal number of documents in the database, including template-only records, chat conversations, and analysed leads.

Templates Sent iWhatsApp template messages sent to phone numbers that have NOT yet started a conversation. These are outreach-only records.

With Chats iLeads who have at least one message in the conversation — meaning the user replied or the AI bot engaged them.
—
Click to view numbers →
Analysed iLeads whose chat conversations have been processed by the AI Lead Qualification Agent. The agent assigns intent, qualification status, and confidence score.
—
Click to view numbers →
Qualified iLeads marked as QUALIFIED by the AI agent — they asked about price, cost, ROI, location, site visit, possession, loan, or EMI.
—
Click to view numbers →
Not Qualified iLeads marked as NOT QUALIFIED — they did not express property buying signals like price inquiries, site visits, or financial questions.
—
Click to view numbers →
Intent Distribution iBreakdown of analysed leads by AI-assigned intent: INTERESTED (asked about property), QUERY (general questions), NOT_INTERESTED, JUNK (spam/greetings only), FAILED (analysis couldn't determine).
Confidence Spread iDistribution of AI confidence scores (0–100%) across analysed leads. Higher confidence means the AI is more certain the lead will convert to a booking. Bucketed into 5 ranges.
Template Campaigns iNumber of WhatsApp template messages sent per project campaign (e.g. KW Delhi 6, KW Blue Pearl). Shows which campaign generated the most outreach.
Qualification Funnel iShows the lead journey from top to bottom: Total documents → Template-only sends → Users who chatted → AI-analysed conversations → Qualified leads. Each bar uses the exact same values as the stat cards above.
